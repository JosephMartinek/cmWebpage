Description

I am a local dance instructor certified in Anne Green Gilbert’s acclaimed creative dance method looking to bring dance into your classroom! The arts, specifically movement and dance, have been scientifically proven to benefit children’s learning as well as their balance, coordination, creativity and self-confidence. As someone with a passion for both dance and early childhood education, I can attest that through my personal experience with the program just how wonderful it is for children’s growing bodies and minds.

I structure my classes into five essential components. The first component is a warm-up, the “Brain Dance”. This is a special set of movements focuses on the body’s natural reflexes and wiring of the brain; it not only warms up the body, but exercises the mind and improves children’s ability to make neuro-connections. The second part of the class is the exploration of the monthly concept, such as how to move through space, parts of the body, musicality and rhythm, and shape. Third is the developing skills component, involving activities to strengthen children’s coordination, balance, and self-control. Fourth is the creating activity of the day, which allows children the ability to explore self-expression and self-discovery. This could include free-dancing involving the introduced concept, an obstacle course, playing with a parachute, dancing along to a movement story, or doing a folk dance. Lastly is a cool-down, involving reflection and relaxing the body.

I can provide:

  • 30 minute or 45 minute lessons
  • My own props
  • My own music
  • Plenty of variety to keep it fun and interesting!
